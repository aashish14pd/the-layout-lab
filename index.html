Overview

What is Physical Design?

Physical Design is the process of transforming a synthesised netlist into a manufacturable silicon layout. It bridges the gap between logical abstraction and silicon reality — ensuring the chip meets every timing, power, and area requirement.

In modern SoC design, PD is one of the most critical phases of the ASIC cycle. Engineers use tools like Synopsys Fusion Compiler, ICC2, and Cadence Innovus to implement designs from floorplanning through GDSII tape-out.

The flow must handle millions of standard cells while satisfying strict constraints on setup/hold timing, IR drop, congestion, and design rule compliance set by the target foundry process node.

Performance

Ensures critical paths meet frequency targets through timing-driven placement and routing.

Power Integrity

Power planning prevents IR drop and electromigration across the full supply network.

Area Optimisation

Efficient floorplanning and placement reduce die area and lower per-unit cost.

DRC / LVS Sign-off

Physical verification ensures the layout obeys fab rules and matches the schematic.

PnR
Place and Route (PnR) is the heart of Physical Design — the automated process of placing millions of standard cells onto the chip core and routing them with metal wires per the netlist. PnR covers Floorplanning, Power Planning, Placement, Clock Tree Synthesis (CTS), Routing, and Static Timing Analysis. It is the bridge from RTL to a silicon-ready GDSII layout, meeting all timing, power, area, and manufacturing constraints.

Physical Verification
Physical Verification is the suite of sign-off checks that confirm a completed layout is both manufacturable and electrically correct. The foundry will not accept a tape-out without clean PV results. It includes DRC — ensuring all shapes comply with foundry manufacturing rules; LVS — confirming the layout matches the schematic; ERC — catching antenna effects, ESD hazards, and latch-up risks; PEX — extracting accurate RC parasitics for post-layout timing; Antenna checks; and final Physical Sign-off across all corners. Every check must pass with zero violations.
